From Italy? We can help!

If you can come on a Wednesday or Sunday...

The nearest airport from Nottingham is EMA and you can flight from pisa on wed and sun. Then take a bus from EMairport and in 45 mins you'll be in the city center [link to skylink]. There are many option like single ticket/ double ticket or family. All the prices are around £5 pp. If you are looking to share a ride with someone will be easier to take an Uber link to app] or call for a taxi from the airport desk [you'll see the indication when you land, don't worry]. The prices are around 20/30£ to reach the city center and it will take less then 30 mins. (be sure to have some change with you if you are planning to take the coach)

...in alternative you can also visit London

You can flight to London almost everyday from pisa/bologna and then take a bus (4 hours ride per £25pp) [link nationalbus] or a train (2 hours ride per £65 pp) [link trainline]. When you grab the bus/train you'll each the city center with no problem. Be sure to have a nice book/music with you.

In Nottingham you'll find a lot of place where to stay during your holidays: there are a bunch of hotel in the city center [link booking] with a good range in prices. Also you can check if you can rent a small flat for you and your familly in the city through the airbnb. app. Be sure to book some place around the city because if you are getting further then NG3 you’ll be pretty far from the center and then you’ll need a bus or a cab to reach the main tourist sites. Also the city it’s quite beautifil and I think you don’t want to miss it!!
